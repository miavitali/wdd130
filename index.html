Buenos Aires

The City of Buenos Aires or Autonomous City of Buenos Aires - also called Federal Capital because it is the seat of the federal government - is the capital of the Argentine Republic. It is located in the central-eastern region of the country, on the western bank of the Río de la Plata, in the heart of the Pampas plain. It is characterized by the presence of plains, meadows, wetlands, lagoons, rivers, sea and mountains that make up the diverse geography of the most populated province in the country.
