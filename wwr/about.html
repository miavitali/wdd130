White Water Rafting

Are you ready for the experience of your life? Well you have come to the right place!! At White Water Rafting we have the mission of bringing to new people an activity that generates sensations and emotions typical of actions that are carried out in open environments and over which we do not have total control, blending the excitement of whitewater rafting with a deep reverence for our planet's beauty. We invite you to find out about our excursions and choose the most appropriate and exciting one for you.

History

White Water Rafting began 30 years ago, born from the shared passion of a group of friends who crave adventure and are outdoor enthusiasts. Since then, we have been dedicated to providing exciting rafting experiences while preserving and raising awareness of the natural beauty around us. Our excursions are aimed at adventurous people over 15 years old who are looking for exciting outdoor experiences and want to venture into a unique and unforgettable experience.
