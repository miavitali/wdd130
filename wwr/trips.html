Trip Cañón Koprulu

Trip Los Dientes del Diablo

Be transported into one of the deepest canyons in the world for a day of scenic drives through the vast Montenegrin countryside and exhilarating walks through the crystal-clear waters of Tara.

Enjoy a wet, rough ride and rafting adventure in the Sleepy River. It is worth a trip alone for its charming rural scenes and the river offers the best spot for rafting.
